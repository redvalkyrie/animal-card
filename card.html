Tiger

Tigers can run at speeds of up to 40mph!

  • Scientific Name: Panthera tigris
  • Average Length: 4.9-9.5 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 20-26 years
  • Habitat: Grasslands, savannas, mangrove swamps, and rainforests

The largest of the cat species, tigers are carnivorous and are most recognizable by their reddish-orange hair and dark verticle stripes. They can weigh up to 670 pounds.
